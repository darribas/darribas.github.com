I am a Senior Lecturer in Geographic Data Science at the Department of Geography and Planning , and member of the Geographic Data Science Lab, at the University of Liverpool (UK), where I direct the MSc in Geographic Data Science.

I am also part of the development team of the open source library PySAL for spatial analysis in Python.

My main research interests are:

  • Geographic Data Science and New Forms of Data
  • Urban Economics and Regional Science
  • Open Source Scientific Computing and Reproducibility
